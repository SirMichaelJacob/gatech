What We do at G.A Technologies

G.A Technologies help entrepreneurs solve some of the problems commonly associated with running a startup by providing workspace, seed funding, mentoring, and training

Comprehensive business training programs

We have a system of activities designed to educate tech founders. It helps them obtain and hone knowledge and skills to progress professionally and personally. develop critical skill sets like leadership, communication and analytical abilities by studying present-day theories. They may also undergo soft skills training to handle ambiguity, lead team-based projects and understand real-world business scenarios.

Networking Opportunities

We organize events that allows tech startups to interact with other business professionals to form connections, seek advice and learn new skills. They interact with potential suppliers or vendors, and have access to industry experts, business competitors and potential clients to start mutually beneficial relationships.

Access to angel investors or venture capital

We help startups develop their products and services and market them to Angel Investors and venture capitals who will become their future partners. This activity is pivotal to launching startups and propelling them to success.
